About

We Provide You With The Best Stock Research

WallStreetBets DD Recommender is a non-profit, completely free project started by two computer science students from Canada. This project aims to be particularly useful towards those, such as ourselves, who are interested in trading and investing in the stock market, but have little time to find or do their own research.

We are filtering out the most valuable posts from r/WallStreetBets by leveraging machine learning (supervised classification based on stock growths and text data) in order to help any busy user quickly filter out what they want to read.

We aim to further democratize stock trading by making the access to insightful stock research easier. Most people already know about the mainstream news outlets, this is why we choose this subreddit, where unique, unpopularized and insightful research are buried in a sea of quality memes.
